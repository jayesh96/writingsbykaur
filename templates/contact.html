About Us

Aim

To help each and everyone find optimism in pessimism. To make you realise your potential and that dreams can come true if you believe in them.

To give you the kind of motivation that you need when life knocks you down and let you read all the stuff that best describes your miseries.To teach women around the globe that YOU TOO CAN DO IT, and give a platform to WOMEN EMPOWERMENT.

Our Goal

To create a society in which words like feminism and petriarchy doesn't exist.A world where people are aware of self-love and have faith in themselves and learn to find their potential.To tell the world who is a KAUR and make each and every women A LIONESS ( A KAUR) by heart.
