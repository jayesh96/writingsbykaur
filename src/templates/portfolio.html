About Us

Writingsbykaur brings together women from all over the world and gives them a platform to showcase their skills, raise their voice against various issues and motivates them to persue their passion.Our motto is to empower women and to prove that women can persue every field. But writingsbykaur is not restricted to women only. We are people who not only wish to, but also works towards bringing a change. We want to make people aware about various serious issues that exists in our society and rescue the victims. Let's work together and remind people about self love and motivation that is burried deep inside them.
